Cooperstown 2026

This summer, our 12U team is heading to the prestigious Cooperstown All Star Village in Cooperstown, New York—the birthplace of baseball. Our players will compete against top teams from across the nation and visit the National Baseball Hall of Fame.

Your generous support helps cover travel expenses, tournament fees, and equipment costs, making this incredible opportunity possible for our young athletes from Hawai'i.
